Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions
Is the program really free?

Yes! All our programs are completely free for underserved communities. We believe every child deserves access to quality coding education.


What ages do you teach?

We have programs for children ages 6-17, with courses tailored to different age groups and skill levels.

Do I need any prior experience?

No prior experience needed! We have beginner courses that start from the very basics.

How do I enroll?

Simply click on the "Book a Course" button in the navigation bar to view our available sessions. Please note that you can only have one enrollment per account..
