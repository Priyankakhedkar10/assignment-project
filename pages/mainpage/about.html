ABOUT   US

Empowering the Next Generation of Coders

Since 2020, TechSpaces has been on a mission to provide high-quality coding education to underserved communities. We believe every child deserves the opportunity to learn the language of the future.



Our Mission

At TechSpaces, we're committed to breaking down barriers in tech education. We provide free, high-quality coding programs that give students the skills, experience, and confidence they need to succeed.

Our teaching philosophy is simple: kids learn to code, by coding. No tutorials or coding games—just real projects, real skills, and real results.


  • Free programs for underserved communities
  • Expert teachers with industry experience
  • Small class sizes for personalized learning
  • Real projects that build portfolios
