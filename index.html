Welcome to TechSpaces

Where learning is serious fun.

We are a registered 501(c)(3) nonprofit that aims to empower underserved kids with the skills, experience, and confidence they need to become successful coders. Our philosophy is simple — kids learn to code, by coding.

About TechSpaces

Kids learn to code, by coding

At TechSpaces, we're committed to providing the highest quality education focused on leadership, innovation, and advanced skills. We don't believe in giving kids coding games or tutorials — our teaching philosophy puts real coding at the center of learning.

  • Hands-on coding projects, not just tutorials

  • Flexible scheduling for busy families

  • Small class sizes for personalized attention

  • Expert teachers with industry experience

  • Free programs for underserved communities

  • Real-world skills that prepare for the future
