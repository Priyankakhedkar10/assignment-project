WELCOME TO CODING


"I turn coffee into code," "Code, Create, Conquer," and "Debugging: Playing Detective".



Coding, or programming, is the process of writing instructions (code) for computers to perform tasks, using specific languages like Python, Java, or C++ to build everything from websites and apps to complex software, acting as the fundamental language that powers modern digital technology and automates processes. It involves creating step-by-step logic (algorithms) that computers can understand and execute, turning human ideas into functional digital products.

The most popular and in-demand languages as of 2026 include:

  • C / C++ / C#: A family of performant languages used in a variety of fields including game development (C# with Unity, C++ for engines), operating systems, and high-performance applications.

  • Python: A versatile, beginner-friendly language used for data analysis, AI/machine learning, and web development.
